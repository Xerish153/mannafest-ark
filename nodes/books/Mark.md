# Mark

The Gospel of the servant in motion. The narrative hinges at Peter's
confession (8:27): before, the Servant revealed; after, the Servant
suffers. Mark's *euthys* ("immediately") pulses roughly forty times
through the chapters — motion without interlude.

Key signature: Mark 10:45 — *"the Son of man came not to be ministered
unto, but to minister, and to give his life a ransom for many."*

Mark 16:9–20 carries a full textual-evidence panel (Burgon 1871, Panin
1934, Irenaeus, Justin Martyr, Tatian, Hippolytus) — verdict: Authentic.
