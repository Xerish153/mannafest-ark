# Acts

The Gospel's sequel. Luke's second volume to Theophilus carries the
witness from the ascension at Jerusalem (chap. 1) through the Pentecost
outpouring (chap. 2), Stephen's martyrdom (chap. 7), Saul's conversion
(chap. 9), Cornelius and the Gentile door (chap. 10), the Jerusalem
Council (chap. 15), Paul's three missionary journeys (chaps. 13–20),
and the voyage to Rome (chaps. 21–28). Acts ends open, with Paul preaching
the kingdom in Rome — the gospel has reached the capital; the story
continues.

Key signature: Acts 1:8 — *"ye shall be witnesses unto me both in
Jerusalem, and in all Judaea, and in Samaria, and unto the uttermost part
of the earth."*
