# Matthew

The Gospel of the promised King. Matthew's narrative is punctuated by five
long teaching discourses, each closed with the refrain *"when Jesus had
finished these sayings"* (7:28; 11:1; 13:53; 19:1; 26:1) — Sermon on the
Mount, Mission of the Twelve, Parables of the Kingdom, Community in the
Kingdom, Olivet Discourse. Roughly sixty-five Old Testament citations
bind the story to the prophets.

Key signature: Matt 28:18–20 — the Great Commission.
