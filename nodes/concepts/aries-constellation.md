# Aries — Taleh

Hebrew *Taleh* (lamb). Bullinger reads Aries as the slain ram — substitutionary atonement imagery. Cross-linked to [[bronze-serpent]] for the substitution typology from a different angle (the wilderness pole episode as type of substitutionary death).

**Biblical anchor (genuinely present):** Gen 22:13 — the ram caught in the thicket, sacrificed in place of Isaac. The ram-as-substitute is a real biblical theme.

**Contested claim:** reading the ram-substitution theme onto the constellation name. The theme is biblical; assigning the constellation a specific gospel-proclamation role is the interpretive move at issue.
