# Ark of the Covenant

The single piece of furniture inside the Most Holy Place (Ex 25:10–16). Acacia wood overlaid with gold **inside and out** (Ex 25:11) — the wood-and-gold "two natures" construction at its purest. 2.5 × 1.5 × 1.5 cubits.

**Contents** (per Heb 9:4):

- **Tablets of the Covenant** — Christ fulfilled the Law (Matt 5:17)
- **Golden pot of manna** — Christ is the bread from heaven (John 6:31–35)
- **Aaron's rod that budded** — Christ's resurrection from death to life (Num 17:8; cf. Acts 2:24)

The Ark disappears from history after Jerusalem's fall to Babylon (587 BC). It is **not** in the Second Temple. Yet **Revelation 11:19** records it being seen in the heavenly temple at the end: *"there was seen in his temple the ark of his testament."*
