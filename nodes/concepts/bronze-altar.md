# Bronze Altar

The first piece of furniture encountered on entering the Tabernacle's Outer Court from the east gate. Five cubits square and three cubits high (Ex 27:1–8) — designed for an animal to be lifted onto and consumed by fire. Bronze symbolizes judgment of sin met head-on; every Israelite entering the sanctuary passed the altar first.

**Christ fulfillment:** the cross (Heb 13:10). The altar is where sacrifice is made before any other worship can proceed.
