# Altar of Incense

Stands immediately before the veil — the closest piece of furniture to the Most Holy Place without crossing into it (Ex 30:1–10). Acacia wood overlaid with gold. Incense burned morning and evening — a perpetual fragrance ascending.

**Christ fulfillment:** *"he ever liveth to make intercession for them"* (Heb 7:25). Rev 5:8 and 8:3–4 render the prayers of the saints themselves as incense before God's throne.
