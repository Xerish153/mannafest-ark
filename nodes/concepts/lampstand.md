# Lampstand (Menorah)

On the south side of the Holy Place, opposite the Table of Showbread (Ex 25:31–40). Hammered from a single talent of pure gold — approximately 75 pounds, formed by beating from one solid piece. Seven branches with almond-blossom cups (Ex 25:33–34) cross-link the menorah to [[trees]] (Aaron's rod, almond imagery).

**Christ fulfillment:** *"I am the light of the world"* (John 8:12; 9:5). Rev 1:12–13 shows Christ standing among seven golden lampstands — the seven churches — as the ongoing pattern.
