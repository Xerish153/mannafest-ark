# Table of Showbread

On the north side of the Holy Place (Ex 25:23–30). Acacia wood overlaid with gold — the recurring "two natures" construction. Twelve loaves of the *bread of the Presence* (Hebrew *lechem ha-panim*, lit. "bread of the face") displayed continually; replaced weekly; the displaced bread eaten by Aaron and his sons.

**Christ fulfillment:** *"I am the bread of life"* (John 6:35, 48) and *"I am the bread which came down from heaven"* (6:41). Christ is the perpetual sustenance of His priestly people in the Holy Place of fellowship.
