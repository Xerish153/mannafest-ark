# Bronze Laver

Stands between the Bronze Altar and the entrance to the Holy Place (Ex 30:17–21; 38:8). Made from the bronze mirrors of the serving women — so that priests, when washing their hands and feet before each approach to God, would see themselves in the metal they were purified at. The fire of judgment behind, the cleansing required to proceed within.

**Christ fulfillment:** cleansing by the Word (Eph 5:26) and baptism (Titus 3:5). Between sacrifice and service, between altar and presence — cleansing.
