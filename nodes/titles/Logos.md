# The Word / Logos

The pre-incarnate Word through whom all things were made — John's
prologue gathering Genesis 1, the personified Wisdom of Proverbs 8,
and the rabbinic Memra tradition into the climactic *"and the Word
was made flesh."*

## Primary anchors
- Gen 1 — creation by divine speech.
- Prov 8:22–31 — Wisdom present at creation.
- Jn 1:1–18 — the prologue of the Word.
- Rev 19:13 — *"his name is called The Word of God."*

## PD sources synthesized
Augustine (*Tractates on John*), Calvin (Jn 1), Westcott (Cambridge
commentary), Henry.
