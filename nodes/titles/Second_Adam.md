# Second Adam / Last Adam

The Last Adam who undoes what the first Adam loosed — Paul's
recapitulation theology of Romans 5 and 1 Corinthians 15 making
Christ the federal head of a renewed humanity.

## Primary anchors
- Gen 3:15 — the *protoevangelium*.
- Rom 5:12–21 — the Adam-Christ parallel.
- 1 Cor 15:22, 15:45–49 — the last Adam, a quickening spirit.
- Heb 2:5–9 — Psalm 8 fulfilled in Jesus.

## PD sources synthesized
Irenaeus (*Against Heresies* V — recapitulation), Augustine (*City
of God* XIII), Calvin (Rom 5).
