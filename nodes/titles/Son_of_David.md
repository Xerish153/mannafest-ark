# Son of David

Messiah qualified by lineage to occupy the throne God promised would
never lack a Davidic heir — and the title under which the Gospel
crowds first acclaimed Jesus.

## Primary anchors
- 2 Sam 7:12–16 — the Davidic covenant.
- Ps 110:1 — David's Lord at YHWH's right hand.
- Mt 1:1, Mt 22:42–45 (Lord-AND-Son puzzle).
- Rev 22:16 — *"the root and the offspring of David."*

## PD sources synthesized
Henry (2 Sam 7, Mt 22), Calvin (Rom 1), Spurgeon (*Treasury of
David* on Ps 110).
