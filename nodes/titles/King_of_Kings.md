# King of Kings

The royal title that closes the Bible — the conquering Lamb whose
name is written on his thigh and whose dominion extends over every
earthly throne.

## Primary anchors
- Deut 10:17 — *"God of gods, and Lord of lords."*
- Dan 2:47, 4:37 — Nebuchadnezzar confesses the Most High.
- 1 Tim 6:15 — *"the blessed and only Potentate."*
- Rev 17:14, 19:16 — the Lamb who conquers.

## PD sources synthesized
Henry (Rev 19, amillennial), Seiss (*The Apocalypse*, premillennial),
Calvin (1 Tim 6 — *only Potentate*). Pre/amillennial readings held in
parallel.
