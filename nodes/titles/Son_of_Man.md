# Son of Man

Jesus's most-used self-designation — at once veiling his identity in
unremarkable Aramaic idiom (*bar enash*) and unveiling it through
Daniel 7's enthroned figure who receives dominion.

## Primary anchors
- Dan 7:13–14 — one like the Son of man receives dominion.
- Mt 26:64 — Daniel-7 self-identification before the Sanhedrin.
- Acts 7:56 — Stephen's vision (sole non-self use).
- Rev 1:13, 14:14 — eschatological appearances.

## PD sources synthesized
Henry (Dan 7), Edersheim (Second-Temple background), Lightfoot
(*Horae Hebraicae*).
