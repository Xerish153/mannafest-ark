# Bright Morning Star

Jesus's self-designation closing the Bible (Rev 22:16) — the Star of
Jacob foretold in Balaam's oracle and the day star of Peter's letter,
rising in the hearts of those who watch for him.

## Primary anchors
- Num 24:17 — Balaam's *"Star out of Jacob, Sceptre out of Israel."*
- 2 Pet 1:19 — *"the day star arise in your hearts."*
- Rev 22:16 — Christ's self-designation closing the Bible.

## PD sources synthesized
Henry (Num 24, Rev 22), Bullinger (*Witness of the Stars*, 1893),
Seiss (*The Gospel in the Stars*, 1882).
