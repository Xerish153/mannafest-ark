# Great High Priest

The title under which Hebrews develops the longest sustained
Christology in the NT — a priesthood superior to the Levitical, after
the order of Melchizedek, offering the once-for-all sacrifice of his
own blood.

## Primary anchors
- Gen 14:18–20 — Melchizedek, priest of the most high God.
- Ps 110:4 — *"a priest for ever after the order of Melchizedek."*
- Heb 4:14–16 — at the throne of grace.
- Heb 7 — the Melchizedek-order argument unfolded.
- Heb 9:11–15 — by his own blood, eternal redemption.

## PD sources synthesized
Owen (*Exposition of Hebrews* — 7 vols, the deepest PD well for this
title), Chrysostom (homilies on Hebrews), Calvin, Henry.
