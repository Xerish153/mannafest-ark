# I AM

The most concentrated divine claim Jesus makes — Ἐγώ εἰμι in
absolute form (John 8:58) and in the seven *"I AM + predicate"*
statements of John, anchored back to the Ex 3:14 disclosure of the
divine name.

## Primary anchors
- Ex 3:14 — *"I AM THAT I AM"* at the burning bush.
- Isa 41:4, 43:10, 43:25, 46:4, 48:12 — *"I am he"* in Isaiah.
- Jn 8:58 — *"Before Abraham was, I am."*
- Jn 18:5–8 — soldiers fall back at Gethsemane.

## Seven "I AM + predicate" statements (John)
6:35 (bread), 8:12 (light), 10:7 (door), 10:11 (shepherd),
11:25 (resurrection), 14:6 (way/truth/life), 15:1 (vine).

## PD sources synthesized
Calvin (Jn 8 — most direct deity-claim in the Gospels), Henry,
Westcott (Cambridge commentary on John), Bullinger (*Names of God*).
