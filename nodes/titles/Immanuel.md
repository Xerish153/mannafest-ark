# Immanuel

The name Matthew places at the start of his Gospel — Isaiah's Hebrew
compound (*Immanu-El*, "with us, God") condensed into the entire
theology of incarnation.

## Primary anchors
- Isa 7:14 — *"behold, a virgin shall conceive."*
- Isa 8:8, 8:10 — the name in possessive and apologetic forms.
- Mt 1:23 — Matthew's opening Christological citation.

## PD sources synthesized
Henry (Isa 7–8), Calvin (Mt 1), Delitzsch (Isaiah commentary).
