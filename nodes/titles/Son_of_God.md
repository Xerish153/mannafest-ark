# Son of God

The eternal Son of the eternal Father — gathering the OT messianic
register of Psalm 2 and Hebrews and the higher claim of homoousios
that the early church confessed at Nicaea.

## Primary anchors
- Ps 2:7 — *"Thou art my Son; this day have I begotten thee."*
- 2 Sam 7:14 — Davidic-covenant Sonship.
- Mt 3:17, Mt 16:16, Mk 1:1, Jn 20:31 — NT declarations.
- Heb 1:5 — Ps 2:7 + 2 Sam 7:14 read together.

## PD sources synthesized
Calvin (Ps 2, Heb 1), Henry (2 Sam 7, 1 Jn 4), Athanasius
(*On the Incarnation*).
