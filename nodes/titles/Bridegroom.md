# Bridegroom

The covenantal-marriage title — Christ as the Husband of his Church,
gathering Hosea, Ezekiel 16, the Song of Solomon, and Ephesians 5
into the eschatological marriage supper of the Lamb.

## Primary anchors
- Hos 2:19–20 — *"I will betroth thee unto me for ever."*
- Ezek 16 — extended marriage metaphor.
- Mt 9:15, Jn 3:29 — Bridegroom self-identification + John Baptist.
- Eph 5:25–32 — Christ-church marriage.
- Rev 19:7–9, 21:2, 22:17 — marriage supper of the Lamb.

## PD sources synthesized
Henry (OT marriage passages, Eph 5, Rev 19), Spurgeon (affective
register), Calvin (Eph 5), Delitzsch (Song of Solomon, literal-
love-poem reading alongside the typological).

## Editorial-posture note
Both Song of Solomon readings held in parallel — Henry + Spurgeon
allegorical-of-Christ-and-church alongside Delitzsch's literal-with-
typological-overlay. Not resolved.
