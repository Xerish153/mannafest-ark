# Alpha and Omega

The first and the last — Jesus speaking in his own voice the divine
self-designation that belongs to YHWH in Isaiah, claimed in
Revelation as both ontological and eschatological encompassing of
all things.

## Primary anchors
- Isa 41:4, 44:6, 48:12 — YHWH's *first-and-last* claims.
- Rev 1:8, 21:6, 22:13 — Christ's self-application of the formula.

## PD sources synthesized
Henry (ontological reading), Seiss + Bullinger (eschatological
reading), Calvin (deity-claim implication). Both readings held in
parallel — not resolved.
