# The Suffering Servant

The title that gathers Isaiah's four Servant Songs into a single
figure — the innocent one whose suffering is vicarious, redemptive,
and accepted by God for many.

## Primary anchors
- Isa 42 · 49 · 50 · 52:13–53:12 — the four Servant Songs.
- Mk 10:45 — *"a ransom for many"* (echoes Isa 53:11–12).
- Acts 8:32–35 — Philip preaches Jesus from Isaiah 53.
- 1 Pet 2:21–25 — Isaiah 53 in pastoral exhortation.

## PD sources synthesized
Calvin (Isa 53), Delitzsch (Isaiah commentary), Henry, Edersheim.
