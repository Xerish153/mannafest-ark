# Good Shepherd

The shepherd-king motif of Ezekiel 34 fulfilled — the one who lays
down his life for the sheep, in deliberate contrast to the
hirelings and false shepherds whom the OT prophets indicted.

## Primary anchors
- Ps 23 — *"The LORD is my shepherd."*
- Ezek 34:23 — the one Davidic shepherd promised.
- Jn 10:11 — *"I am the good shepherd."*
- Heb 13:20 — the great shepherd of the sheep.
- 1 Pet 5:4 — the chief Shepherd.

## PD sources synthesized
Spurgeon (*Treasury of David* on Ps 23), Henry (Ezek 34, Jn 10),
Calvin, Owen (echoes of *great* in Heb).
