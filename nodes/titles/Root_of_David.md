# Root of David / Lion of Judah

The two titles announced together at Revelation 5:5 — the Lion of
Judah promised in Jacob's blessing of Genesis 49, and the Root of
David that is at once before and after David's line.

## Primary anchors
- Gen 49:9–10 — Lion of Judah; the sceptre until Shiloh.
- Num 24:17 — Star out of Jacob.
- Isa 11:1, 11:10 — rod from Jesse's stem; root of Jesse.
- Rev 5:5 — *"the Lion of the tribe of Juda, the Root of David."*
- Rev 22:16 — *"I am the root and the offspring of David."*

## The Lion-Lamb paradox
Rev 5:5 announces the *Lion*; Rev 5:6 displays *"a Lamb as it had
been slain."* Seiss treats the announcement-and-appearance pattern
as deliberately pedagogical — the Lion announced is the slain Lamb
seen, and the slain Lamb seen is the Lion who has prevailed.

## PD sources synthesized
Henry (Gen 49, Rev 22), Bullinger (*The Apocalypse* — root-and-
