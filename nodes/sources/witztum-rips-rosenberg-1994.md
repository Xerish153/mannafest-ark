# Witztum, Rips, Rosenberg 1994

Doron Witztum, Eliyahu Rips, and Yoav Rosenberg, *"Equidistant Letter Sequences in the Book of Genesis,"* **Statistical Science** 9(3): 429–438 (1994). Peer-reviewed paper proposing statistically significant ELS patterns in the Hebrew text of Genesis — most notably the Torah YHWH skip-49 pattern and proximity-based ELS matches for rabbinic-figure names with their dates.

**On MannaFest:** cited as the academic anchor for the Torah YHWH pattern rendered in [[trees]] depth-2 §5 (ELS introduction per Vision §7.10). The paper is publicly citable; the text is not reproduced beyond the journal attribution.

**Counter-voice:** [[mckay-et-al-1999]] — McKay, Bar-Natan, Bar-Hillel, Kalai responded in the same journal (*Statistical Science* 14(2), 1999) with the "Solving the Bible Code Puzzle" randomization critique. Both papers are part of the peer-reviewed record and cited side-by-side.
