# McKay et al. 1999

Brendan McKay, Dror Bar-Natan, Maya Bar-Hillel, and Gil Kalai, *"Solving the Bible Code Puzzle,"* **Statistical Science** 14(2) (1999). Peer-reviewed response to [[witztum-rips-rosenberg-1994]]. Argues that comparable ELS patterns can be produced in randomized texts — including Tolstoy's *War and Peace* and other non-sacred works — and that the original Witztum-Rips-Rosenberg findings rest on insufficiently constrained methodology (the search space, the figure-set selection, the date-format choices).
