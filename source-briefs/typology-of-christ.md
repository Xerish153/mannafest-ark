## Page intent

The master hub for typology on MannaFest. Catalogs ~46 distinct types of Christ across five categories (Persons, Events, Objects & Institutions, Offices, Feasts), each with clear scriptural warrant. Functions as the gravitational center connecting to every typology-tagged feature page, verse page, and character profile on the site. A student should be able to see the pattern at a glance, filter by category, and drill into any type.

Distinct from Seed Promise and Scarlet Thread: those are *threads* (linear arcs). This is a *hub* (density catalog). Where a type has its own feature page, this hub delegates rather than duplicates.
