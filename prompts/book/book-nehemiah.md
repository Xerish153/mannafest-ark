# Book — Nehemiah

**Slug:** `nehemiah` · **Tier:** 2 · **Chapters:** 13 · **Signature verse:** Neh 8:8
**Shipped:** Wave D · Batch 20 (2026-04-23)

## Identity

Walls rebuilt in fifty-two days; covenant renewed; reforms enacted then re-enacted when Nehemiah returns to find them slipping. About civic and spiritual reconstruction side by side.

## Framework (Tier 2 template)

- Historical setting: Persian period under Artaxerxes; Nehemiah's first term 445–433 BC; second term c. 432 BC.
- Literary structure: walls rebuilt (1–7); covenant renewed under Ezra (8–10); community organized, second reform (11–13).
- Theological contribution: prayer interleaved with action; "they read distinctly, and gave the sense" (8:8) as synagogue-teaching precedent; reform as ongoing not one-time.
