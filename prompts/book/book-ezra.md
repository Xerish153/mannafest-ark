## Identity

The return in two waves — Zerubbabel's altar and temple (538 BC) and Ezra's reforming arrival (458 BC). About rebuilding the temple, the Torah-teaching community, and the covenant boundary.

## Framework (Tier 2 template)

- Historical setting: Persian period; Cyrus's decree 538 BC; Ezra arrives 458 BC.
- Literary structure: first return under Zerubbabel (1–6); Ezra's return and covenant reform (7–10).
- Theological contribution: Ezra's threefold commitment (7:10 — seek, do, teach); covenant boundary as post-exilic discipline.
