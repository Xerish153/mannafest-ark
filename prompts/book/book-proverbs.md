## Identity

Gnomic wisdom — sayings, instructions, and the binary of wisdom vs. folly. Fear of the LORD as the integrative theme; the virtuous woman of Prov 31 as the book's closing portrait of wisdom embodied.

## Framework (Tier 2 template)

- Historical setting: Solomon's reign c. 970–930 BC; Hezekiah's editorial layer c. 715–687 BC (Prov 25:1).
- Literary structure: long discourses (1–9) + Solomonic proverbs proper (10–24) + Hezekiah's men's collection (25–29) + Agur (30) + Lemuel and the virtuous woman (31).
- Theological contribution: fear-of-the-LORD as integrative theme (Prov 1:7 + 9:10 + 31:30 bookends and midpoint); wisdom personified (Prov 8) as foundation for later Christ-as-Wisdom theology.
