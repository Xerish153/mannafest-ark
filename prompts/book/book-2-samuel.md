## Identity

David's reign in three movements — rise (1–10), the Davidic covenant (ch. 7 as spine), consequences (11–24). A covenant held through both rise and fall; house, throne, kingdom forever.

## Framework sections (depth 2)

- The Davidic Covenant (ch. 7) — Psalm 89, Psalm 132, Matt 1:1 "Jesus Christ, the son of David." Primary hub-side anchor for the Covenants feature page.
- Jerusalem as the City of David — threshing floor of Araunah (24:24) = Mount Moriah (2 Chr 3:1) = Gen 22 Akedah site.
- The Bathsheba narrative — Psalm 51 as David's response; the biblical historiography's unflinching treatment.
- Absalom's rebellion — Sheba's "we have no part in David" (20:1) as the exact phrase Jeroboam echoes in 1 Kgs 12:16.
