## Identity

Solomon to the exile retold through the southern kings only. A priestly history that keeps its lens on the temple, on reform kings, and on the repentance formula (7:14) that will be quoted in a thousand sermons.

## Framework (Tier 2 template)

- Historical setting: post-exilic, c. 450–400 BC.
- Literary structure: Solomon (1–9); kings of Judah Rehoboam → Ahaz (10–28); Hezekiah through exile (29–36).
- Theological contribution: the repentance formula (7:14); Manasseh's repentance (33:10–17, unique to Chronicles); hope for the returnee community.
