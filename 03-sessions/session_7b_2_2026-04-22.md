`featured_page_slug='suffering-servant-title'` to avoid collision with
the existing Batch 6 seed (`featured_page_slug='suffering-servant'`,
`route_prefix='/study'`, pointing at the Wave-2 feature page). But the
shipped `<FeaturedStudiesOnVerse />` constructs href as
`${route_prefix}/${featured_page_slug}` — so the unique slug would
have produced `/title/suffering-servant-title` which doesn't match
the title's actual route `/title/suffering-servant`. The component
also dedupes by slug alone, so re-using the canonical slug
`suffering-servant` would dedupe-mask one of the two cards on Isa 53.

registration for `suffering-servant` entirely. Discovery for the
title page comes via the Wave-2 callout in the body, plus the
`/titles` cluster hub. The dedupe behavior is a Batch 7-B
component-level concern; a future fix would dedupe by
(route_prefix, slug). Flagged for later hygiene.
